How do I buy? 
          1. Download Trust Wallet and set everything up
          2. From the TrustWallet app click on DApps
          3. Either type in, "PancakeSwap" in the search bar or scroll down to the popular section and click on
          PancakeSwap
          4. Once on PancakeSwap click, "Connect Wallet", and then click on Trust Wallet when it asks which wallet you'd
          like to connect. Your wallet should now be connected and you can use your BNB in your Trust Wallet to trade
          for DOBO on PancakeSwap
          5. Either buy BNB on Trust Wallet with a credit card or buy it on another exchange like Crypto.com and send it
          to your Trust Wallet.
          6. Once the BNB is in your Trust Wallet make sure its BNB Smart Chain. If its regular BNB then click on DEX
          within Trust Wallet to swap your regular BNB for BNB Smart Chain. The symbol for regular BNB is gold and white
          while the symbol for BNB Smart Chain is gold and black. If you open up PancakeSwap while only holding regular
          BNB you will not be able to use it to buy DOBO.
          7. Once you have your BNB Smart Chain open up PancakeSwap
          8. Have the "From" section be set to BNB. In the, "To" section click on, "Select a currency" and in the search
          bar put in this contract number: 0xae2df9f730c54400934c06a17462c41c08a06ed8
          9. Once you've put it in click the button that looks like a gear in the top right corner. Go down to slippage
          tolerance and type, "11"
          10. Exit out of the settings and enter the amount of BNB you want to swap for DOBO. You can only buy
          5,000,000,000,000 DOBO per transaction. If you try to buy more at once it wont work
          11. Click on, "Swap" and then click, "Confirm Swap" and then click, "Approve". If this doesn't work or you get
          an error try rounding the amount of BNB you wanted to trade for DOBO to a whole number. For example, if you
          tried to trade 11.45653 BNB for DOBO and got an error, then change the amount of BNB to 11. If this still
          doesn't work then try increasing the slippage by 1% until it works.
